CSS

I wanted my pictures to spin when you hovered over them. I chose this because it felt fun and whimsical I did this by going into the index css and adding "transition: width 2s, height 2s, background-color 2s, rotate 2s, transform 1s;" into the .picture and "rotate: 360 deg;" into .picture:hover. This made the pictures spin when you hover over them.
